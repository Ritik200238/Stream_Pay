## Recording Tips:

1. **Screen Resolution**: 1920x1080 for best quality
2. **Recording Tool**: OBS Studio (free) or Windows Game Bar (Win+G)
3. **Audio**: Use a decent microphone, quiet room
4. **Cursor**: Keep it visible when pointing things out
5. **Pace**: Speak clearly, not too fast
6. **Practice**: Run through once before final recording
7. **File Format**: MP4 (most compatible)
